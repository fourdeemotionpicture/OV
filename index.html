THE PROCESS

MADE IN SMALL BATCHES

STEP 01

ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN

Every fit is drafted individually, balancing shoulder drops with strict vertical sleeve cuts to form the ultimate boxy shape.

STEP 02

PRECISION CUTTING

Double-combed 100% cotton panels are cut with laser precision to prevent warp or sizing variation between batches.

STEP 03

DEEP GARMENT DYE

Dyed post-stitch inside specialized high-pressure drums. This ensures washed texture edges and vintage color depths.

STEP 04

HANDPACKED LOGISTICS

Pieces are lint-brushed, packed inside raw organic canvas dust bags, and wrapped inside custom matte silver boxes.
